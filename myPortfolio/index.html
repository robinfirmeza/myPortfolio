About Me

Hello! I’m Robin D. Firmeza, a BS Computer Science student from Iloilo Science and Technology University (ISAT-U) with a strong passion for technology, problem-solving, and building digital solutions that make everyday tasks easier. I enjoy turning ideas into functional systems—whether it’s a mobile ticketing app, a route optimization tool, or a user-friendly web platform.
